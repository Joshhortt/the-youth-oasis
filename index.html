You might be thinking, Setting Travel Goals? That’s a waste of time, why would I do that?
I just find the best price and decide where to go from there. And while we believe in taking
advantage of deals and last-minute pricing too, that’s not exactly what we’re talking about.
Holidays or vacations are words that bring joy to everyone but they mean different things to
different people. For some people it would mean fishing in your uncle’s boat or visiting your
grandma’s place or simply lazing around in some beach. For me it means travel to a exotic
country and look around, absorb the culture try out the local food and have fun along the way.
